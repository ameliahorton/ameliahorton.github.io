Amelia Horton


Amelia Horton is a musician, chef, and data scientist. Originally from West Point, New York, she holds a bachelor's degree in Trumpet from Oberlin Conservatory of Music. She also has a variety of experiences in food service, and currently works as an assistant pastry chef. Alongside working, she studies Data Science at Northwestern University and hopes that her multidisciplinary background will be an asset as she seeks employment in the field.
